Dear OHS Class of 2019 Student,

This is your invite to upload your senior portrait that you would like for us to use the in 2019 OHS Yearbook.

Some of the rules:

  • The photo is to be comprised of you, no props please. No other people are allowed in the photo either.
  • No animals or any other type of pets are allowed either. If you would like to feature them please purchase a senior ad.
  • No laying down, please be facing the camera vertically.
  • Both your eyes should be visible and face uncovered.

If your photo doesn't meet these requirements it may be denied and you will need to resubmit a different photo.

All photos are due by October 31, 2018.

Please click the button below to upload your yearbook photo. It takes less than 2 minutes.

Thank you. If you have any questions please email them to mzarate@osd.wednet.edu.
